Chapter 1: The Awakening Flame

Just as Kiyanotoka was celebrating his new Spirit Beast—a Baby Fire Dragon—a familiar voice echoed in the distance.

"You bastard, Ash! Get over here!"

It was Brad, storming toward him. His breathing was heavy. While muscular, his sheer bulk made it hard for him to run far.

Brad's spiritual beast had awakened when he was young. In this world, spirit beasts are tied to the heart—their rank and form can't be altered once bonded. But Kiyanotoka, with his mysterious System, was... irregular.

Chapter 2: The Real Show

Kiyanotoka’s System scanned Brad’s beast—the Dusk Wolf Cub, a shadow-type spirit beast. It wasn’t weak, but against Thamuz, the Fire Dragon, it stood little chance.

System Scan – Target: Brad
Spirit Pet: Dusk Wolf Cub
Element: Shadow / Darkness
Skills: Shadow Pounce, Flicker Hide, Whimper Howl

Brad launched the first attack. But the system-assisted defense from Thamuz quickly turned the battle around.

Chapter 3: First Clash – Fire vs. Shadow

The alley lit with flame and shadow. Brad’s beast attacked from the darkness, but Thamuz repelled it with Blazing Scale Burst. Kiyanotoka commanded calmly, and every action felt like clockwork.

Victory
Beast Defeated – +1 Kill
Progress to Dragon Evolution: 1 / 10

And with the system’s help, a new opportunity emerged—his own skill path.

Chapter 4: The Silent Commander

On the rooftop, Kiyanotoka chose his skill path in silence:

"I don't want to be the strongest… I want to lead the strongest."
Skill Acquired: Tamer’s Command – Level 1
+1 Beast Slot
Title: Initiate Commander
System Hidden from External Scans

At the Spirit Appraisal Grounds, he showed only what he wanted the world to see. While others suspected something odd, none could pinpoint the truth.

Chapter 5: Into the Scorchwood Ridge

Kiyanotoka formed his squad—Fuyue Lin, Riku Kaen, and Maya Jin—and entered the deadly Scorchwood Ridge. With perfect coordination, they defeated Firetail Apes, then delved deeper.

Optional Challenge Unlocked: Defeat B-Rank Boss
Reward: Fire Crystal Shard

They took down the Molten Horned Lizard through strategy, trust, and silent commands. And the system rewarded them:

Tamer’s Command → Level 2
Beast Evolution Material Acquired

Chapter 6: Echoes Beyond the Trial

Squad 7 returned victorious and drew the eyes of the academy. Kiyanotoka, still hiding his system, kept a low profile while planning his next bond.

Trait Quest: Commander's Domain (Bond 2/3 Required)
Objective: Form second bond
Beast Evolution: 12% Awakening

And under the light of the moon, he found a second spirit beast—a Lunar Sylph-Hare. Gentle, elusive, and powerful.

He welcomed it quietly, like a true commander working beneath the world’s notice.
